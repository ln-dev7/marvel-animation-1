Aquamyst

Aquamyst wields the power of water with grace and agility, her costume reflecting the hues of the deepest seas. She manipulates water to form both shield and weapon in battle.

Infernox

Infernox commands the fierce energy of fire, his costume ablaze with red and orange. Flames envelop his limbs, enhancing his fiery attacks.

Terraforce

Strong and unyielding, Terraforce embodies the strength of the earth. His rugged appearance is complemented by stone-like textures, symbolizing his rock-solid powers.

Aerowind

Aerowind is the essence of the air element, her costume light and ethereal. She moves with the grace of a gentle breeze but can summon the force of a tempest.
